What is burnout?

Burnout is a state of physical and emotional exhaustion. It can occur when you experience long-term stress in your job, or when you have worked in a physically or emotionally draining role for a long time.

What are the causes of burnout?

Burnout is not caused only because of stressful work or overwhelming responsibilities, other factors lead to burnout as well, including your lifestyle, personality traits and your downtime activities.

How can you deal with burnout?

Three symptoms characterize burnout: exhaustion, cynicism and inefficacy. Aim to prioritize your health, take a break, reduce workload as much as possible and take your time for rest and renewaw.

Our Mission

We are PaveMind, a team of six motivated students whose biggest motivation is to help others with their everyday life by raising awareness on the topic of burnout.

We created a safe space for everyone who wants to share their experience, read more on how to cope, find valuable resources and track their progress. Our team finds this topic extremely relevant and wants to help others with their journey, educate them more on the topic and create a meaningful product that will be beneficial.
